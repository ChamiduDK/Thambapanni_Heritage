Sri Lankan Wood Carving Heritage

The wood carving tradition of Sri Lanka represents centuries of craftsmanship, featuring intricate designs and spiritual significance. From ancient temple carvings to traditional masks, each piece tells a unique story.

The ancient craftsmen of Sri Lanka developed sophisticated techniques for working with native woods like Teak, Mahogany, and Ebony, creating masterpieces that have stood the test of time.

These wooden sculptures showcase the perfect blend of artistic excellence and cultural symbolism, making Sri Lankan wood carving one of the most respected traditional art forms in Asia.
